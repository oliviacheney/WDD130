Overview

Purpose

Have you ever gone online to find a recipe and haven’t been able to tell if the recipe was good or not? Sure, some people leave reviews rating a specific dish but a lot of these reviews are just people looking at the recipe, not people who have made it and know all the ins and outs of it. My website plan is to have my own food blog, but rather than only posting recipes I create and come up with myself, I make other people’s dishes from other blogs and post about the experiences on my website. By doing this, people can know in depth if a certain recipe is the best.

Audience

Everyone who is looking for delicious and easy recipes that they know are going to be a homerun.
